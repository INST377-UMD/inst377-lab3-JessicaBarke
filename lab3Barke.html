Tips

Strategy

Paper beats Rock, Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper.

How to Play

  1. Both players choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. Rock crushes Scissors.
  3. Scissors cut Paper.
  4. Paper covers Rock.
  5. Same choice = tie; play again.
